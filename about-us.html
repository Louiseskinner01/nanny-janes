About Nanny Janes Nursery

Providing a safe, nurturing and inspiring environment for children for over 35 years.

Nanny Janes Nursery is a family-run childcare setting dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing and stimulating environment where children can learn, grow and thrive.

We believe that the early years are the most important stage in a child’s development. Our experienced team creates a welcoming environment where children feel secure, valued and encouraged to explore the world around them.

Through play-based learning and carefully planned activities, children develop the confidence, curiosity and social skills that will support them throughout their education and beyond.

Our Founder

Nanny Janes Nursery was founded by Jane Ann, a respected member of the local community with over 35 years of childcare experience.

Jane’s vision was to create a nursery that feels like a home away from home, where children feel supported, encouraged and inspired every day.

Her passion for early years education and commitment to providing high-quality childcare has helped build lasting relationships with families throughout the community.

Today, Nanny Janes Nursery continues to reflect Jane’s dedication to creating a warm, safe and enriching environment where every child can reach their full potential.
