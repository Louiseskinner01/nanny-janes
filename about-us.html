About Nanny Janes Nursery

Providing a safe, nurturing and inspiring environment for children for over 35 years.

At Nanny Janes Nursery, we are a family-run childcare setting committed to providing a safe, nurturing, and inspiring environment where every child can learn, grow, and thrive. We understand that the early years are a vital stage in a child’s development. That is why our dedicated and experienced team works closely with each child to support their individual needs, interests, and abilities. Our goal is to create a welcoming space where children feel happy, secure, and valued every day. We follow a play-based approach to learning, encouraging children to explore, discover, and develop naturally through engaging activities and experiences. By combining structured learning with creative play, we help children build confidence, curiosity, independence, and strong social skills.
At Nanny Janes Nursery, we believe that strong partnerships with parents are essential. We work closely with families to ensure every child receives the care, support, and encouragement they need during these important early years. Our nursery is more than just a place for childcare – it is a place where children build friendships, develop a love for learning, and create the foundations for a bright and successful future.

Our Founder

Nanny Janes Nursery was founded by Jane Ann, a respected member of the local community with over 35 years of experience in childcare. Jane’s vision was to create a nursery that truly feels like a home away from home — a place where children feel safe, supported, encouraged, and inspired every day. Her passion for early years education, combined with her dedication to providing high-quality childcare, has helped build strong and lasting relationships with families throughout the community. Today, Nanny Janes Nursery continues to reflect Jane’s values and commitment to creating a warm, safe, and enriching environment where every child is supported to grow, learn, and reach their full potential.
